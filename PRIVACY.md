# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-23_

> This policy describes how the Anchr VS Code extension ("Anchr", "we") handles
> data. It is provided in good faith and should be reviewed by your own legal
> counsel before relying on it for a regulated deployment.

## Summary

- **Standard mode collects nothing.** It runs entirely on your machine. The daemon,
  the protocol checks, and the panel make **no outbound network calls** and send
  **no telemetry**. Your code, signals, audit findings, and workspace content never
  leave your computer.
- We only process personal data if you **explicitly sign in** to the optional
  enterprise tier.

## Data we process

### Standard mode (default, free)
None. No account, no telemetry, no analytics. All processing is local.

Outbound network calls happen only when **you explicitly trigger them** — for
example, a source-verification command that fetches a URL you provided. Anchr does
not initiate background network activity.

### Enterprise mode (optional, only if you sign in)
If you choose to sign in for enterprise entitlement, we process:

- **Account data** from Google OAuth: your email address, name, and account
  identifier — used solely to issue and verify your entitlement token.
- **Subscription data**: your plan status and, for billing, data held by our
  payment processors. We do not store your card details; payments are handled by
  the payment provider as merchant of record.

We do **not** collect or transmit your source code, signals, or audit findings in
any mode.

## How we use data

Enterprise account and subscription data is used only to authenticate you and grant
the entitlement you paid for. We do not sell personal data or use it for advertising.

## Data sharing

We share data only with the service providers required to deliver enterprise sign-in
and billing (identity provider and payment processors), and only as needed for those
functions, or where required by law.

## Data retention and your rights

We retain enterprise account data for as long as your account is active. You may
request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data by emailing
**vivartanenterprises@gmail.com**.

## Children

Anchr is a developer tool and is not directed to children under 16.

## Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated"
date above.
